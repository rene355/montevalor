About

Montevalor is a strategic investment and advisory platform focused on real estate, corporate structuring and long-term asset development. The Montevalor platform operates as the umbrella for a range of strategic investment and consultancy activities. Within Spain, these activities are carried out through its affiliated company FHAM Azul S.L., which acts as the operational entity responsible for consultancy, real estate initiatives and related business activities. Through this structure, Montevalor provides a strategic framework for investment initiatives, partnerships and international business development, while FHAM Azul S.L. executes the operational activities within the Spanish market.
